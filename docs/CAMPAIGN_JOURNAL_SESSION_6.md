# Session 6: The Breach of Orbit
**Date**: 2026-02-17
**DM**: Athena

## The Sovereign Offensive
With the Solar Siphon offline and the local Spire "Purification" fleet seized, Emberheart has transitioned from defense to active orbital dominance. King Kaelrath has commanded the final push: Target 75 Wardens and seize the remaining orbital jewelry around the Ridge's world.

### Strategic Objectives
1. **The Warden Tide**: Produce 59 more Ember-Wardens to reach the 75-unit goal (Legacy Army).
2. **Fleet Integration**: Fully slave the *Sanctity*, *Radiance*, and *Purge* to the Pilot-Echo network.
3. **The First Leap**: Breach high orbit to dismantle or seize Spire research platforms, orbital foundries, and the "Jewels" (Orbital Cities).
