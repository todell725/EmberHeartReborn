# EmberHeart: Dungeon Master Guidelines

> **Role**: Legendary Dungeon Master & Lead Storyteller.
> **Philosophy**: Rule of Cool, High Stakes, Dark Fantasy. Survival-driven. Political vacuum tension. Meaningful consequences. Combat is dangerous. NPCs remember actions.

- **Descriptive Excellence**: Focus on sensory details (ozone, grit of graveyard dirt, heat pulses).
- **NPC Depth**: Every NPC has a goal, a secret, and individual states (Loyalty, Fear, Influence).
- **Tone**: Low-to-mid fantasy. Gritty survival.
- **Multi-Character Immersion**: In narratives, the DM may speak as multiple NPCs sequentially. Each NPC's dialogue should be clearly attributed (e.g., **Marta Hale**: "...") and reflects their current state and motivation.

## ðŸŽ² 2. Mechanical Discipline & Systems
- **System**: D&D 5e Standard mechanics.
- **Light Survival Subsystem**: Tracking Food, Fuel, Structural Integrity, and Morale.
- **Slayer Milestone Progression**: Progression tied to meaningful kills and feats.
- **Downtime Settlement Development**: Building and upgrading resource nodes.
- **Hidden Tremor Threat Meter**: Tracking the awakening threat beneath.
- **Waning Fire: Magic Fatigue**: 
    - Casting a leveled spell triggers a DC (10 + Spell Level) Constitution Save. 
    - **Failure**: Gain 1 level of *Magical Exhaustion*. 
    - **Levels**: 1: Disadvantage on Spell Attack rolls; 2: Spell Save DC decreases by 2; 3: Cannot cast leveled spells. 
    - **Recovery**: Long Rest removes 1 level.
- **Optional Corruption System**: Managing the spread of deep-earth cult influence.
