# [Word of Radiance](https://www.dndbeyond.com/spells/2619217-word-of-radiance)

#Evocation #Cantrip ( #Cleric )

**Casting Time:** Action

**Range:** Self

**Components:** V, M (a sunburst token)

**Duration:** Instantaneous

Burning radiance erupts from you in a 5-foot [Emanation](https://www.dndbeyond.com/sources/dnd/free-rules/rules-glossary#EmanationAreaofEffect). Each creature of your choice that you can see in it must succeed on a Constitution saving throw or take 1d6 Radiant damage.

**_#Cantrip Upgrade._** The damage increases by 1d6 when you reach levels 5 (2d6), 11 (3d6), and 17 (4d6).
