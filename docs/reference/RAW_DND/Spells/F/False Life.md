# [False Life](https://www.dndbeyond.com/spells/2618866-false-life)

#Level1 #Necromancy ( #Sorcerer, #Wizard)

**Casting Time:** Action

**Range:** Self

**Components:** V, S, M (a drop of alcohol)

**Duration:** Instantaneous

You gain 2d4 + 4 [Temporary Hit Points](https://www.dndbeyond.com/sources/dnd/free-rules/rules-glossary#TemporaryHitPoints).

**_Using a Higher-Level Spell Slot._** You gain 5 additional Temporary Hit Points for each spell slot level above 1.
