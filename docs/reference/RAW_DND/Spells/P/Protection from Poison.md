# [Protection from Poison](https://www.dndbeyond.com/spells/2618915-protection-from-poison)

#Level2 #Abjuration ( #Cleric, #Druid, #Paladin, #Ranger)

**Casting Time:** Action

**Range:** Touch

**Components:** V, S

**Duration:** 1 hour

You touch a creature and end the [Poisoned](https://www.dndbeyond.com/sources/dnd/free-rules/rules-glossary#PoisonedCondition) condition on it. For the duration, the target has [Advantage](https://www.dndbeyond.com/sources/dnd/free-rules/rules-glossary#Advantage) on saving throws to avoid or end the Poisoned condition, and it has Resistance to Poison damage.
