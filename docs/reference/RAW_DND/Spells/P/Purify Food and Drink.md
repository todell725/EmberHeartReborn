# [Purify Food and Drink](https://www.dndbeyond.com/spells/2618917-purify-food-and-drink)

#Level1 #Transmutation ( #Cleric, #Druid, #Paladin)

**Casting Time:** Action or Ritual

**Range:** 10 feet

**Components:** V, S

**Duration:** Instantaneous

You remove poison and rot from nonmagical food and drink in a 5-foot-radius [Sphere](https://www.dndbeyond.com/sources/dnd/free-rules/rules-glossary#SphereAreaofEffect) centered on a point within range.
