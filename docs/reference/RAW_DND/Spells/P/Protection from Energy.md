# [Protection from Energy](https://www.dndbeyond.com/spells/2618908-protection-from-energy)

#Level3 #Abjuration ( #Cleric, #Druid, #Ranger, #Sorcerer, #Wizard)

**Casting Time:** Action

**Range:** Touch

**Components:** V, S

**Duration:** [Concentration](https://www.dndbeyond.com/sources/dnd/free-rules/rules-glossary#Concentration), up to 1 hour

For the duration, the willing creature you touch has Resistance to one damage type of your choice: Acid, Cold, Fire, Lightning, or Thunder.
