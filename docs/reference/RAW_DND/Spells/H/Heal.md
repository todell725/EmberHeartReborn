# [Heal](https://www.dndbeyond.com/spells/2618979-heal)

#Level6 #Abjuration ( #Cleric, #Druid)

**Casting Time:** Action

**Range:** 60 feet

**Components:** V, S

**Duration:** Instantaneous

Choose a creature that you can see within range. Positive energy washes through the target, restoring 70 Hit Points. This spell also ends the [Blinded](https://www.dndbeyond.com/sources/dnd/free-rules/rules-glossary#BlindedCondition), [Deafened](https://www.dndbeyond.com/sources/dnd/free-rules/rules-glossary#DeafenedCondition), and [Poisoned](https://www.dndbeyond.com/sources/dnd/free-rules/rules-glossary#PoisonedCondition) conditions on the target.

**_Using a Higher-Level Spell Slot._** The healing increases by 10 for each spell slot level above 6.
