# [Hold Monster](https://www.dndbeyond.com/spells/2619152-hold-monster)

#Level5 #Enchantment ( #Bard, #Sorcerer, #Warlock, #Wizard)

**Casting Time:** Action

**Range:** 90 feet

**Components:** V, S, M (a straight piece of iron)

**Duration:** [Concentration](https://www.dndbeyond.com/sources/dnd/free-rules/rules-glossary#Concentration), up to 1 minute

Choose a creature that you can see within range. The target must succeed on a Wisdom saving throw or have the [Paralyzed](https://www.dndbeyond.com/sources/dnd/free-rules/rules-glossary#ParalyzedCondition) condition for the duration. At the end of each of its turns, the target repeats the save, ending the spell on itself on a success.

**_Using a Higher-Level Spell Slot._** You can target one additional creature for each spell slot level above 5.
