# [Destructive Wave](https://www.dndbeyond.com/spells/2619089-destructive-wave)

#Level5 #Evocation ( #Paladin )

**Casting Time:** Action

**Range:** Self

**Components:** V

**Duration:** Instantaneous

Destructive energy ripples outward from you in a 30-foot [Emanation](https://www.dndbeyond.com/sources/dnd/free-rules/rules-glossary#EmanationAreaofEffect). Each creature you choose in the Emanation makes a Constitution saving throw. On a failed save, a target takes 5d6 Thunder damage and 5d6 Radiant or Necrotic damage (your choice) and has the [Prone](https://www.dndbeyond.com/sources/dnd/free-rules/rules-glossary#ProneCondition) condition. On a successful save, a target takes half as much damage only.
