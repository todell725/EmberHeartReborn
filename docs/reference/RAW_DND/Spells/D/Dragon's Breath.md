# [Dragon’s Breath](https://www.dndbeyond.com/spells/2619150-dragons-breath)

#Level2 #Transmutation ( #Sorcerer, #Wizard)

**Casting Time:** Bonus Action

**Range:** Touch

**Components:** V, S, M (a hot pepper)

**Duration:** [Concentration](https://www.dndbeyond.com/sources/dnd/free-rules/rules-glossary#Concentration), up to 1 minute

You touch one willing creature, and choose Acid, Cold, Fire, Lightning, or Poison. Until the spell ends, the target can take a [Magic](https://www.dndbeyond.com/sources/dnd/free-rules/rules-glossary#MagicAction) action to exhale a 15-foot [Cone](https://www.dndbeyond.com/sources/dnd/free-rules/rules-glossary#ConeAreaofEffect). Each creature in that area makes a Dexterity saving throw, taking 3d6 damage of the chosen type on a failed save or half as much damage on a successful one.

**_Using a Higher-Level Spell Slot._** The damage increases by 1d6 for each spell slot level above 2.
