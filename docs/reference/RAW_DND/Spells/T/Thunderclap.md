# [Thunderclap](https://www.dndbeyond.com/spells/2619179-thunderclap)

#Evocation #Cantrip ( #Bard, #Druid, #Sorcerer, #Warlock, #Wizard)

**Casting Time:** Action

**Range:** Self

**Components:** S

**Duration:** Instantaneous

Each creature in a 5-foot [Emanation](https://www.dndbeyond.com/sources/dnd/free-rules/rules-glossary#EmanationAreaofEffect) originating from you must succeed on a Constitution saving throw or take 1d6 Thunder damage. The spell’s thunderous sound can be heard up to 100 feet away.

**_#Cantrip Upgrade._** The damage increases by 1d6 when you reach levels 5 (2d6), 11 (3d6), and 17 (4d6).
