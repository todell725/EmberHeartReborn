# [Vicious Mockery](https://www.dndbeyond.com/spells/2619216-vicious-mockery)

#Enchantment #Cantrip ( #Bard )

**Casting Time:** Action

**Range:** 60 feet

**Components:** V

**Duration:** Instantaneous

You unleash a string of insults laced with subtle enchantments at one creature you can see or hear within range. The target must succeed on a Wisdom saving throw or take 1d6 Psychic damage and have [Disadvantage](https://www.dndbeyond.com/sources/dnd/free-rules/rules-glossary#Disadvantage) on the next attack roll it makes before the end of its next turn.

**_#Cantrip Upgrade._** The damage increases by 1d6 when you reach levels 5 (2d6), 11 (3d6), and 17 (4d6).
