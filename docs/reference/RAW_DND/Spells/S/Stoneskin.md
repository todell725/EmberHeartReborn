# [Stoneskin](https://www.dndbeyond.com/spells/2619095-stoneskin)

#Level4 #Transmutation ( #Druid, #Ranger, #Sorcerer, #Wizard)

**Casting Time:** Action

**Range:** Touch

**Components:** V, S, M (diamond dust worth 100+ GP, which the spell consumes)

**Duration:** [Concentration](https://www.dndbeyond.com/sources/dnd/free-rules/rules-glossary#Concentration), up to 1 hour

Until the spell ends, one willing creature you touch has Resistance to Bludgeoning, Piercing, and Slashing damage.
