# [Spike Growth](https://www.dndbeyond.com/spells/2619075-spike-growth)

#Level2 #Transmutation ( #Druid, #Ranger)

**Casting Time:** Action

**Range:** 150 feet

**Components:** V, S, M (seven thorns)

**Duration:** [Concentration](https://www.dndbeyond.com/sources/dnd/free-rules/rules-glossary#Concentration), up to 10 minutes

The ground in a 20-foot-radius [Sphere](https://www.dndbeyond.com/sources/dnd/free-rules/rules-glossary#SphereAreaofEffect) centered on a point within range sprouts hard spikes and thorns. The area becomes [Difficult Terrain](https://www.dndbeyond.com/sources/dnd/free-rules/rules-glossary#DifficultTerrain) for the duration. When a creature moves into or within the area, it takes 2d4 Piercing damage for every 5 feet it travels.

The transformation of the ground is camouflaged to look natural. Any creature that can’t see the area when the spell is cast must take a [Search](https://www.dndbeyond.com/sources/dnd/free-rules/rules-glossary#SearchAction) action and succeed on a Wisdom ([Perception](https://www.dndbeyond.com/sources/dnd/free-rules/playing-the-game#Skills) or [Survival](https://www.dndbeyond.com/sources/dnd/free-rules/playing-the-game#Skills)) check against your spell save DC to recognize the terrain as hazardous before entering it.
