# [Sleet Storm](https://www.dndbeyond.com/spells/2618998-sleet-storm)

#Level3 #Conjuration ( #Druid, #Sorcerer, #Wizard)

**Casting Time:** Action

**Range:** 150 feet

**Components:** V, S, M (a miniature umbrella)

**Duration:** [Concentration](https://www.dndbeyond.com/sources/dnd/free-rules/rules-glossary#Concentration), up to 1 minute

Until the spell ends, sleet falls in a 40-foot-tall, 20-foot-radius [Cylinder](https://www.dndbeyond.com/sources/dnd/free-rules/rules-glossary#CylinderAreaofEffect) centered on a point you choose within range. The area is [Heavily Obscured](https://www.dndbeyond.com/sources/dnd/free-rules/rules-glossary#HeavilyObscured), and exposed flames in the area are doused.

Ground in the Cylinder is [Difficult Terrain](https://www.dndbeyond.com/sources/dnd/free-rules/rules-glossary#DifficultTerrain). When a creature enters the Cylinder for the first time on a turn or starts its turn there, it must succeed on a Dexterity saving throw or have the [Prone](https://www.dndbeyond.com/sources/dnd/free-rules/rules-glossary#ProneCondition) condition and lose [Concentration](https://www.dndbeyond.com/sources/dnd/free-rules/rules-glossary#Concentration).
