# [Staggering Smite](https://www.dndbeyond.com/spells/2619084-staggering-smite)

#Level4 #Enchantment ( #Paladin )

**Casting Time:** Bonus Action, which you take immediately after hitting a creature with a Melee weapon or an Unarmed Strike

**Range:** Self

**Component:** V

**Duration:** Instantaneous

The target takes an extra 4d6 Psychic damage from the attack, and the target must succeed on a Wisdom saving throw or have the [Stunned](https://www.dndbeyond.com/sources/dnd/free-rules/rules-glossary#StunnedCondition) condition until the end of your next turn.

**_Using a Higher-Level Spell Slot._** The extra damage increases by 1d6 for each spell slot level above 4.
