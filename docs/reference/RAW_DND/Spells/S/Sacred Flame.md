# [Sacred Flame](https://www.dndbeyond.com/spells/2618967-sacred-flame)

#Evocation #Cantrip ( #Cleric )

**Casting Time:** Action

**Range:** 60 feet

**Components:** V, S

**Duration:** Instantaneous

Flame-like radiance descends on a creature that you can see within range. The target must succeed on a Dexterity saving throw or take 1d8 Radiant damage. The target gains no benefit from [Half Cover](https://www.dndbeyond.com/sources/dnd/free-rules/rules-glossary#Cover) or [Three-Quarters Cover](https://www.dndbeyond.com/sources/dnd/free-rules/rules-glossary#Cover) for this save.

**_Cantrip Upgrade._** The damage increases by 1d8 when you reach levels 5 (2d8), 11 (3d8), and 17 (4d8).
