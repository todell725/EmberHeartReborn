# [Silence](https://www.dndbeyond.com/spells/2619061-silence)

#Level2 #Illusion ( #Bard, #Cleric, #Ranger)

**Casting Time:** Action or Ritual

**Range:** 120 feet

**Components:** V, S

**Duration:** [Concentration](https://www.dndbeyond.com/sources/dnd/free-rules/rules-glossary#Concentration), up to 10 minutes

For the duration, no sound can be created within or pass through a 20-foot-radius [Sphere](https://www.dndbeyond.com/sources/dnd/free-rules/rules-glossary#SphereAreaofEffect) centered on a point you choose within range. Any creature or object entirely inside the Sphere has Immunity to Thunder damage, and creatures have the [Deafened](https://www.dndbeyond.com/sources/dnd/free-rules/rules-glossary#DeafenedCondition) condition while entirely inside it. Casting a spell that includes a Verbal component is impossible there.
