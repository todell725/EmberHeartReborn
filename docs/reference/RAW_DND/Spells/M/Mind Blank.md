# [Mind Blank](https://www.dndbeyond.com/spells/2619036-mind-blank)

#Level8 #Abjuration ( #Bard, #Wizard)

**Casting Time:** Action

**Range:** Touch

**Components:** V, S

**Duration:** 24 hours

Until the spell ends, one willing creature you touch has Immunity to Psychic damage and the [Charmed](https://www.dndbeyond.com/sources/dnd/free-rules/rules-glossary#CharmedCondition) condition. The target is also unaffected by anything that would sense its emotions or alignment, read its thoughts, or magically detect its location, and no spell—not even [Wish](https://www.dndbeyond.com/spells/2619213-wish)—can gather information about the target, observe it remotely, or control its mind.
