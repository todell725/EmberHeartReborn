# [Ensnaring Strike](https://www.dndbeyond.com/spells/2619172-ensnaring-strike)

#Level1 #Conjuration ( #Ranger )

**Casting Time:** Bonus Action, which you take immediately after hitting a creature with a weapon

**Range:** Self

**Components:** V

**Duration:** [Concentration](https://www.dndbeyond.com/sources/dnd/free-rules/rules-glossary#Concentration), up to 1 minute

As you hit the target, grasping vines appear on it, and it makes a Strength saving throw. A Large or larger creature has [Advantage](https://www.dndbeyond.com/sources/dnd/free-rules/rules-glossary#Advantage) on this save. On a failed save, the target has the [Restrained](https://www.dndbeyond.com/sources/dnd/free-rules/rules-glossary#RestrainedCondition) condition until the spell ends. On a successful save, the vines shrivel away, and the spell ends.

While Restrained, the target takes 1d6 Piercing damage at the start of each of its turns. The target or a creature within reach of it can take an action to make a Strength ([Athletics](https://www.dndbeyond.com/sources/dnd/free-rules/playing-the-game#Skills)) check against your spell save DC. On a success, the spell ends.

**_Using a Higher-Level Spell Slot._** The damage increases by 1d6 for each spell slot level above 1.
