# [Enthrall](https://www.dndbeyond.com/spells/2619192-enthrall)

#Level2 #Enchantment ( #Bard, #Warlock)

**Casting Time:** Action

**Range:** 60 feet

**Components:** V, S

**Duration:** [Concentration](https://www.dndbeyond.com/sources/dnd/free-rules/rules-glossary#Concentration), up to 1 minute

You weave a distracting string of words, causing creatures of your choice that you can see within range to make a Wisdom saving throw. Any creature you or your companions are fighting automatically succeeds on this save. On a failed save, a target has a −10 penalty to Wisdom ([Perception](https://www.dndbeyond.com/sources/dnd/free-rules/playing-the-game#Skills)) checks and [Passive Perception](https://www.dndbeyond.com/sources/dnd/free-rules/rules-glossary#PassivePerception) until the spell ends.
