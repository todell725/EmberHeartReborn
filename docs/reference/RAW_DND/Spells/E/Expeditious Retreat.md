# [Expeditious Retreat](https://www.dndbeyond.com/spells/2619189-expeditious-retreat)

#Level1 #Transmutation ( #Sorcerer , #Warlock, #Wizard)

**Casting Time:** Bonus Action

**Range:** Self

**Components:** V, S

**Duration:** [Concentration](https://www.dndbeyond.com/sources/dnd/free-rules/rules-glossary#Concentration), up to 10 minutes

You take the [Dash](https://www.dndbeyond.com/sources/dnd/free-rules/rules-glossary#DashAction) action, and until the spell ends, you can take that action again as a Bonus Action.
