# [Lupine Mask](https://www.dndbeyond.com/magic-items/9445280-lupine-mask)

#Wondrous *item,* #Uncommon 

While wearing this dark ceramic mask, you have advantage on Wisdom ([Perception](https://www.dndbeyond.com/sources/dnd/free-rules/playing-the-game#Skills)) checks relying on hearing and smell, as well as on Wisdom ([Animal Handling](https://www.dndbeyond.com/sources/dnd/free-rules/playing-the-game#Skills)) checks made to interact with dogs and dog-like beasts, such as [foxes](https://www.dndbeyond.com/monsters/1123082-fox), [jackals](https://www.dndbeyond.com/monsters/5195091-jackal), and [wolves](https://www.dndbeyond.com/monsters/4775850-wolf).
