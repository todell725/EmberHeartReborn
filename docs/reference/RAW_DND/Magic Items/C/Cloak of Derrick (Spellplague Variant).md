# Cloak of Derrick ([[Spellplague]] Variant)

#Wondrous *item,* #Legendary 

This magnificent robe was the height of fashion across the [[Sword Coast]] after the time of the [[Spellplague]]. Its decadent burlap material is highlighted by varying shades of astonishing beige and luxurious dark beige.

While you are wearing this cloak, you gain a +4 bonus to all attack rolls, saving throws, and ability checks, as well as to your Armor Class. 

**Dutiful Defender.** Once per Long Rest, you can cast [[Power Word Fortify]] through the cloak. 

**Dazzling Disguise.** Once per Long Rest, you can cast [[Shapechange]] through the cloak.
