# [Focus Circlet](https://www.dndbeyond.com/magic-items/9445138-focus-circlet)

#Wondrous *item,* #Uncommon *(requires attunement by a spellcaster)*

While wearing this silver circlet, you gain a bonus to Constitution saving throws that you make to maintain your [concentration](https://www.dndbeyond.com/sources/basic-rules/spellcasting#Concentration) on a spell you cast equal to the spell’s level, up to a maximum of +3.
