# Scepter of Grim Psychometry

#Staff, Rarity Undetermined *(Requires Attunement by a Sorcerer, Warlock, or Wizard)*

**Spells.** This staff should for sure have spells from the school of #Divination . Select spells of an appropriate level for the party that receives this item, then put them into the table below. Use the [[Staff of Power]] or a similar staff as a template for this table.

| **Spell** | **Charge Cost** |
| --------- | --------------- |
|           |                 |


**Grim Psychometry.** This scepter grants its wielder a supernatural proclivity for discerning secrets surrounding mysterious relics or places touched by evil. Once per Long Rest, you can attempt to recall information about the sinister or tragic history of an object you are touching or your current location. Roll 1d100 and consult the following table:

| **1d100** | **Result**                                                                                                                                                                                                                    |
| --------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 1-10      | Sinister whispers fill your ears, swirling through your consciousness. You take 2d12 Psychic damage.                                                                                                                          |
| 11-74     | You receive a momentary vision depicting a single, vague image related to the past of the object or location                                                                                                                  |
| 75-100    | You experience an evocative vision of the past. This vision reveals critical information about the object or location in question, which is described at the DM's discretion. Once this vision ends, you feel physically ill. |
