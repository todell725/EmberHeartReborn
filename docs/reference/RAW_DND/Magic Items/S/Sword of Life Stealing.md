# [Sword of Life Stealing](https://www.dndbeyond.com/magic-items/9229113-sword-of-life-stealing)

#Weapon *(Glaive, Greatsword, Longsword, Rapier, Scimitar, or Shortsword),* #Rare *(Requires Attunement)*

When you attack a creature with this magic weapon and roll a 20 on the d20 for the attack roll, that target takes an extra 15 Necrotic damage if it isn’t a Construct or an Undead, and you gain [Temporary Hit Points](https://www.dndbeyond.com/sources/dnd/free-rules/rules-glossary#TemporaryHitPoints) equal to the amount of Necrotic damage taken.
