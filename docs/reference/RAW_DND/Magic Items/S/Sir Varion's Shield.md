# [Sir Varion’s Shield](https://www.dndbeyond.com/magic-items/9445500-sir-varions-shield)

#Armor *(shield),* #Uncommon  *(requires attunement)*

This large shield once belonged to Sir Varion, a noble knight who strode bravely through battles on ice, sand, or swamp. While holding this shield, you ignore nonmagical [difficult terrain](https://www.dndbeyond.com/sources/basic-rules/adventuring#DifficultTerrain) and have a +1 bonus to AC. This bonus is in addition to the shield’s normal bonus to AC.
