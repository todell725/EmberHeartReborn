# Amulet of Yenna's Protection

#Wondrous *Item*, #Rare *(Requires Attunement)*

As a Reaction, this amulet can either...

1. Cancel the extra damage of a critical hit on anyone within 30 ft. of you, including yourself
2. Halve the damage of an attack made against anyone within 30 ft. of you, including yourself

You can use **one** of these effects once per Long Rest.
