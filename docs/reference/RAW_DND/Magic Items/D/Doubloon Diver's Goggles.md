# [Doubloon Diver’s Goggles](https://www.dndbeyond.com/magic-items/9445092-doubloon-divers-goggles)

#Wondrous *item,* #Uncommon 

These leather and shell-covered goggles have peculiarly narrow lenses. While wearing the goggles, you have [darkvision](https://www.dndbeyond.com/sources/dnd/free-rules/rules-glossary#Darkvision) out to a range of 120 feet while underwater. In addition, you have advantage on Wisdom ([Perception](https://www.dndbeyond.com/sources/dnd/free-rules/playing-the-game#Skills)) and Intelligence ([Investigation](https://www.dndbeyond.com/sources/dnd/free-rules/playing-the-game#Skills)) checks you make that rely on sight while underwater.
