# [Turtle Brooch](https://www.dndbeyond.com/magic-items/9445555-turtle-brooch)

#Wondrous *item,* #Common 

This gold and amber brooch resembles a turtle that’s been flipped on its back. While you’re wearing it, standing up from being [prone](https://www.dndbeyond.com/sources/dnd/free-rules/rules-glossary#ProneCondition) costs 5 feet less of your movement (to a minimum of 5 feet).
