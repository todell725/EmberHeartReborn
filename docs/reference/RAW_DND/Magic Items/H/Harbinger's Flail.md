# [Harbinger’s Flail](https://www.dndbeyond.com/magic-items/9445194-harbingers-flail)

#Weapon *(flail),* #Rare 

This whip-like chain flail’s head is a sonorous bell from the Temple of Twin Fates, a holy site known for its grim foretellings. This weapon has the [reach](https://www.dndbeyond.com/sources/dnd/free-rules/equipment#Reach)property and deals an extra 1d4 thunder damage to any target it hits. When it hits a target, the bell releases a deep sound that’s audible out to 100 feet.
