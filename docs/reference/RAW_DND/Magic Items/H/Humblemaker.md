#Weapon *(any hammer),* *Rarity Varies* *(Requires Attunement)*

*Humblemaker* is a thick, rune-inscribed maul forged from celestial bronze and star-forged iron. Its haft is wrapped in stormhide leather, and the twin heads of the hammer glow faintly with a metallic sheen. Despite its massive size, it feels perfectly balanced in the hands of one who is attuned to it. The hammer is said to be a leftover relic of an ancient civilization of Giants who have long since died out.


***Phase 1. ( #Uncommon )*** You gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made using the weapon. On a hit, the weapon deals an extra 1d6 Force damage.

***Phase 2. ( #Rare )*** The Phase 2 weapon has the Phase 1 property. *Humblemaker* grows in size by 1 foot. When you attack a creature with the hammer and roll a 20 on the d20 for the attack roll, the hammer deals an extra 15 Force damage.

***Phase 3. ( #VeryRare )*** The Phase 3 weapon has the Phase 2 property, and it improves on the Phase 1 property. *Humblemaker* grows in size by 3 feet. While attuned to this hammer, your Strength score increases by 2, to a maximum of 22. The bonus to attack and damage rolls increases to +2, and the extra Force damage dealt by the weapon increases to 2d6.

Once per long rest when you hit a target with *Humblemaker*, the head of the hammer vibrates with terrible purpose creating a calamitous shockwave. Allies make this saving throw with Advantage. On a failure, the creature takes 10d6 Force damage and is knocked prone. On a success, the creature takes half damage only.

***Phase 4. ( #Legendary )*** The Phase 4 weapon has the Phase 3 property, and it improves on the Phase 2 and Phase 1 properties. *Humblemaker* grows in size by 5 feet.  The bonus to attack and damage rolls increases to +3, and the extra Force damage dealt by the weapon increases to 3d6.

The calamitous shockwave created by the hammer increases to a 40-foot [Sphere](https://www.dndbeyond.com/sources/dnd/free-rules/rules-glossary#SphereAreaofEffect), the save DC increases to 18, and the damage increases to 14d6.
