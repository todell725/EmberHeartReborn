#Weapon *(blowgun),* #Uncommon *(requires attunement)*

This magic, twisting blowgun channels the air around it to create powerful blasts of wind. When you hit with a ranged attack using this weapon, the target takes an extra 1d4 bludgeoning damage, and if the target is a Medium or smaller creature, it must succeed on a DC 13 Strength saving throw or be pushed 5 feet away from you.
