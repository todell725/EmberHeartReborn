# [Potion of Diminution](https://www.dndbeyond.com/magic-items/4703-potion-of-diminution)

#Potion, #Rare

When you drink this potion, you gain the “reduce” effect of the [[Enlarge Reduce]] spell for 1d4 hours (no [Concentration](https://www.dndbeyond.com/sources/dnd/free-rules/rules-glossary#Concentration)required).

The red in the potion’s liquid continuously contracts to a tiny bead and then expands to color the clear liquid around it. Shaking the bottle fails to interrupt this process.
