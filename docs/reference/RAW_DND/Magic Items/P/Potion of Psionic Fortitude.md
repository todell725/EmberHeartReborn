# [Potion of Psionic Fortitude](https://www.dndbeyond.com/magic-items/7247086-potion-of-psionic-fortitude)

#Potion, #Uncommon 

When you drink this potion, you have advantage for 1 hour on saving throws you make to avoid or end the [charmed](https://www.dndbeyond.com/sources/dnd/free-rules/rules-glossary#CharmedCondition) or [stunned](https://www.dndbeyond.com/sources/dnd/free-rules/rules-glossary#StunnedCondition) condition on yourself.

This black potion swirls with shimmering flecks of pink and purple.
