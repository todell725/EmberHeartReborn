# [Potion of Growth](https://www.dndbeyond.com/magic-items/4707-potion-of-growth)

#Potion, #Uncommon

When you drink this potion, you gain the “enlarge” effect of the [[Enlarge Reduce]] spell for 10 minutes (no [Concentration](https://www.dndbeyond.com/sources/dnd/free-rules/rules-glossary#Concentration)required).

The red in the potion’s liquid continuously expands from a tiny bead to color the clear liquid around it and then contracts. Shaking the bottle fails to interrupt this process.
