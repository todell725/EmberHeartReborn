# [Wraps of Unarmed Power](https://www.dndbeyond.com/magic-items/9229208-wraps-of-unarmed-power)

#Wondrous *Item*, #Uncommon *(+1),* #Rare *(+2), or* #VeryRare *(+3)*

While wearing these wraps, you have a bonus to attack rolls and damage rolls made with your [Unarmed Strikes](https://www.dndbeyond.com/sources/dnd/free-rules/rules-glossary#UnarmedStrike). The bonus is determined by the wraps’ rarity, and those strikes deal your choice of Force damage or their normal damage type.
