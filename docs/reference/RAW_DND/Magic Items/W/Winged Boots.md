# [Winged Boots](https://www.dndbeyond.com/magic-items/9229203-winged-boots)

#Wondrous *Item*, #Uncommon *(Requires Attunement)*

These boots have 4 charges and regain 1d4 expended charges daily at dawn. While wearing the boots, you can take a [Magic](https://www.dndbeyond.com/sources/dnd/free-rules/rules-glossary#MagicAction) action to expend 1 charge, gaining a Fly Speed of 30 feet for 1 hour. If you are flying when the duration expires, you descend at a rate of 30 feet per round until you land.
