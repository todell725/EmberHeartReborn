# [Walloping Ammunition](https://www.dndbeyond.com/magic-items/36839-walloping-ammunition)

#Weapon *(Any Ammunition),* #Common

A creature hit by this ammunition must succeed on a DC 10 Strength saving throw or have the [Prone](https://www.dndbeyond.com/sources/dnd/free-rules/rules-glossary#ProneCondition) condition.
