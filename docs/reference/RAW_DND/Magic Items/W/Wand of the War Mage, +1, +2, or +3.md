# [Wand of the War Mage, +1, +2, or +3](https://www.dndbeyond.com/magic-items/34712-wand-of-the-war-mage)

#Wand, #Uncommon *(+1),* #Rare *(+2), or* #VeryRare *(+3) (Requires Attunement by a Spellcaster)*

While holding this wand, you gain a bonus to spell attack rolls determined by the wand’s rarity. In addition, you ignore [Half Cover](https://www.dndbeyond.com/sources/dnd/free-rules/rules-glossary#Cover) when making a spell attack roll.
