# Faction Rules: EmberHeart
Version: 1.0

## Faction Pressure (0-20)

### 1. Iron Concordat
- **Growth**: Tied to Mining OU yield and Mine Depth.
- **Threshold 7**: Soft attention.
- **Threshold 10**: Envoy/Ultimatum regarding Shaft 3 or records.

### 2. Kingdom of Valemere
- **Growth**: Tied to Population and Trade Visibility.
- **Threshold 7**: Scout activity near borders.
- **Threshold 14**: Economic sanctions or sovereignty claims.

### 3. Ashen Theocracy
- **Growth**: Tied to Geothermal Rituals and Shrine Influence.
- **Threshold 7**: Religious tension.

### 4. Verdant Accord
- **Growth**: Tied to Ecological Disruption and Corruption Signals.

## 贸易影响 (Economic Impact)
- Pressure >= 10: Exchange rates worsen by 25%.
- Pressure >= 18: Caravans stop or are faction-controlled.

## 介入规则 (Intervention)
Athena must not trigger war or annexation without Tier 4 user choice.
